Travel App Case Study

Connecting Urban Travellers with locals

Overview Research Problem Statement Prototype

PROJECT SUMMARY

The goal of this project was to create a digital product to improve the experience of travellers in a post-pandemic world.

Responsibilities

UX/UI Designer (Individual Project)

Timeline

15 Nov - 15 Dec 2022

It is easy for travellers to feel overwhelmed with the unfamiliarity of a place and the amount of decisions to be made without having sincere recommendations from locals. How might we build a product that connects travellers with like-minded locals who are passionate about recommending their favourite spots or are generous with their time curating tours to travellers.
